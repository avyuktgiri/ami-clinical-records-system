Minimal Preprocessing (Medical Data Integrity)

Because this is clinical data, we avoid aggressive cleaning that could distort medically important patterns.
  • No synthetic filling of values (dataset already has no nulls).
  • Removed exact duplicates to reduce repeated-sample bias.
  • Applied feature scaling for stable model training.
  • Avoided over-editing outliers to preserve clinical realism.
